Tools for your reader

Two small tools for a paper-first reader.

Pick one. The installer flashes Paperloom firmware onto a LilyGo T5 e-paper board over USB. The optimizer trims EPUBs down to the 16-gray e-ink palette before you upload them — all in your browser, nothing leaves your machine.

Available tools

Firmware

Install firmware

Flash the latest Paperloom build onto a LilyGo T5 E-Paper S3 Pro or Pro Lite. USB-C cable, one minute, no drivers.

→ Books

Optimize an EPUB

Quantize, dither, and strip down EPUBs for the 16-level e-ink display. Drop a file, get a smaller, sharper one back. Your books never leave the browser.

→

Why two tools

Paperloom's firmware is small and offline. Anything that doesn't have to run on the device shouldn't — and most of the work in preparing a book for an e-ink screen is mechanical: shrink images, drop fonts, fix the TOC. The optimizer does that work in your browser, so the reader stays a reader.
